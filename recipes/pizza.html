Pizza

What you need to make a Pizza

Don't you just love a well done pizza on a friday night while watching a movie? Yess! You will love this simple pizza recipe instead of ordering delivery.

If you have flour, yeast and a few other kitchen basics you're already halfway there!

Ingredients

  • Yeast: 2 1/4 teaspoons
  • All purpose unbleached white flour: 1 standard sized bag
  • Water: 1 1/3 cups of warm water
  • Extra virgin oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Salt: 1 tablespoon
  • Sugar: 1 tablespoon
  • Cornmeal: A good sprinkle

Steps

  1. Mix all the Ingredients by hand or a mixer whichever you prefer.
  2. Knead by hand or by mixer. Personally I prefer by hand.
  3. Place your dough into mixing bowl, cover tightly and set aside for about 90 minutes or into a refrigerator overnight.
  4. Punch the dough down to release the possible air bubbles that have formed. Split into however much dough you need for your prefered size.
  5. Cover it with all your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake pizza at about 450 degrees for about 15 minutes.
