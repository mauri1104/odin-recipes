Burger

What you need to make a Burger

Don't you just love the taste of a nice charboile burger on a nice summer day?

It's easier to cook then you may think with the right ingredients

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patties
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Charcoal Grill
  • Any condiments and toppings you personally like on your burger

Steps

  1. Create about 8oz ground beef if they're not already that size
  2. Heat your grill up to about 500 degrees
  3. Add some salt and pepper to both sides of the patty then let it sit for about 15 mins while the grill finishes heating up
  4. Once your charcoal starts to burn down into a gray ash form you can add your patty
  5. Grill your burger for about 3 minutes on each side for medium well with the lid open then closed for another 2 minutes
  6. Remove the burgers and place them on the buns immediately
  7. Add all the condiments and toppings you love and enjoy your Charbroiled burger!
